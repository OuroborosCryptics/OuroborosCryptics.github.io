Ouroboros Cryptics

Hi there.

I'm Ouroboros, a cryptic setter from the UK. While my grids are not yet found

in any physical publications, I aim to change that in the near future. This

site is still largely a work in progress, but will be updated with new grids on

a frequent basis. While I currently have no strict uploading schedule, I'm

aiming for around one new grid a week. Grids found here include standard 15x15s

(which may or may not contain themes), Jumbo 21x21 grids which generally include

either thematic surfaces or entries, and my own creation - a non-themed 4x4 which

I've dubbed the "Cryptitchy".


Most of my grids are aimed at experienced solvers, so don't feel too disheartened

if you find yourself hitting a wall. I aim to add some beginner-level grids in

the future, and possibly even publish some beginner's guides. I'm have recently

expanded into barred 13x13s, with one grid now online. These barred grids will

(naturally) include rare and obscure words, as well as special instructions

governing the solving and entering of clues.
