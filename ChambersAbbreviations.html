All Chambers Abbreviations

This page lists all one, two and three-letter abbreviations found in Chambers Dictionary,

13th Edition. This list has been compiled with zero regard for whether the abbreviation is

capitalised or not; for most clues this won't matter, but care should be taken when creating

reverse-wordplay clues. Some abbreviations work for capital and lower-case letters (e.g. A

and a are both abbreviations for "acre"). In such instances, the word will appear only once

Notes concerning the use of abbreviations have been largely omitted for ease of reading.

Four-letter abbreviations are not included, nor are abbreviations that include ampersands.



A

absolute (temperature); Academician; Academy; ace (cards); acre(s); adult; advanced;

alto (music); amateur; America; American; ammeter; angstrom; answer; area; argon (now Ar);

Associate (of a society, etc); Athletic; atomic; atomic weight; Australia; Australian;

Austria (IVR); about; absent; acceleration; accepted; acreage; acting; active; adjective;

advance; afternoon; anno (L), in the year; annus (L), year; anonymous; ante (L), before;

anterior; are (metric measure); atto-.



AA

Alcoholics Anonymous; anti-aircraft; Architectural Association; Associate of Arts; Australian

Army; Automobile Association.



AAA

Amateur Athletic Association; American Automobile Association; anti-aircraft artillery;

Australian Automobile Association.



AAC

American Athletic Club; anno ante Christum (L), in the year before Christ; Army Air Corps.



AAM

air-to-air missile.



AAP

Australian Associated Press.



AAR

against all risks (commerce); average annual rainfall.



AAS

Academiae Americanae Socius (L), Fellow of the American Academy.



AAT

alpha-1 antitrypsin, a tissue-protecting blood protein.



AB

able-bodied (seaman); Advisory Board; Alberta (Canadian province); Artium

Baccalaureus (L), Bachelor of Arts (US); Autobahn.



ABA

Amateur Boxing Association; American Bar Association.



ABB

Abbess; Abbey; Abbot.



ABC

American Broadcasting Company; Arab Banking Corporation; Associated

British Cinemas; Audit Bureau of Circulations; Australian Broadcasting

Corporation (formerly, Commission)
